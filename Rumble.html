About Rumble

Rumble is a video-sharing platform that positions itself as an alternative to YouTube, emphasizing free speech and creator rights. Founded in 2013 by Chris Pavlovski, Rumble has grown significantly and provides content creators with better monetization opportunities and fewer restrictions on content.

Platform Features

Key Advantages

  • Better revenue sharing for creators
  • Less restrictive content policies
  • Built-in monetization tools
  • Enhanced copyright protection
  • Transparent algorithms
